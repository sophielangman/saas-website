Our Service

Comfort of Home

With StethaSkype you can take care of all your medical business from home. Video chat with your doctor or specialist, get online prescriptions, learn how to do self check-ups with our additional apps and instruments and more. Never again will you need to travel long distance for health care, health care will come to you.

We’ve Got the Pros

Canada is home to some of the best medical professionals in the world, but it’s also the second largest country in the world making it a difficult to actually access these resources. Not anymore, we’ll help you reach out nation wide and find the perfect doctor to treat you.

We Work for You

Our accounts are built to be customized. Keep track of your records, bills, doctors and diagnostics all in one place. Keep personalized notes and reminders and communicate with your medical practitioners regularly. We’ll even recommend useful articles and videos specific to you.

Skip the Waiting Room

You’ll never need to waste a minute in the waiting room again, plan your appointments ahead of time to the minute and we’ll find a time that works for you. Plus with our online features you decide the time and the place.
